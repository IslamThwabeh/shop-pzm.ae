PZM retail design standard

Direction
- Clean retail UI, not glassmorphism.
- White surfaces, restrained gradients, thin borders, soft shadows, and generous but controlled spacing.
- Product and category media should never dominate the viewport on mobile unless it is an intentional hero banner.

Sizing benchmark
- Card media height on mobile: 160px to 200px.
- Card media height on tablet: 184px to 208px.
- Card media height on desktop: 208px to 240px.
- Feature panel media on mobile: around 172px.
- Feature panel media on desktop: around 232px.
- Standard content width: 1280px max with responsive gutters.

Layout rules
- Default grid progression: 1 column mobile, 2 columns tablet, 3 columns desktop.
- Use 4 columns only for simple, high-scan cards on wide desktops.
- Avoid forced tall aspect-ratio cards for catalog and service browsing.
- Section spacing should feel calm, not oversized: 64px mobile, 76px tablet, 88px desktop.

Card rules
- Product cards: white background, thin border, soft shadow, 20px to 24px radius.
- Product imagery: framed on white or near-white background, object-contain when selling a device, object-cover when showing environment or service photography.
- Card padding: 20px mobile, 24px desktop.

Typography rules
- Keep retail copy compact and scannable.
- Avoid oversized blog or service headings on mobile.
- Body text should usually sit around 15px to 16px on content-heavy pages.

Theme tokens
- Source of truth for React app: frontend/src/index.css.
- Legacy static CSS must mirror the same shadows, spacing rhythm, and media caps.

Do not use
- Heavy glassmorphism panels.
- Full-viewport card images in catalog sections.
- Decorative gradients or shadows that compete with product discovery.
